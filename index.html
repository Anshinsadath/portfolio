About Me

Who am I?

I am a Computer Science graduate with a strong foundation in front-end web development, specializing in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Alongside web development, I have gained hands-on experience in building responsive and user-friendly mobile app interfaces using Flutter. My technical journey has been shaped by both academic learning and practical exposure to real-world projects.

During my internship as a Junior Frontend Developer, I contributed to the development of a Learning Management System (LMS) platform, where I enhanced the front-end design and functionality to improve user experience. This opportunity allowed me to apply my skills in a collaborative environment and strengthened my understanding of building scalable and efficient applications.

Beyond professional experience, I have led impactful academic projects that integrate innovation and emerging technologies. My recent work includes developing a Q&A NLP chatbot for electric cars using Python, Django, and Gemini AI, as well as creating an AI-powered SaaS startup guidance platform that leverages social media insights for trend analysis and strategy optimization. Passionate about seamless user experiences and AI-driven solutions, I strive to combine creativity, technology, and problem-solving to deliver meaningful results.
